Brief

What is The Workshop?

The prospect community for BALANZI. A private channel from the founders — the vision, decisions, mistakes, and the answers a careful buyer would want before purchasing. Not VIP. Not perks. Just the work.

Architecture: page captures email only · invite link arrives via Klaviyo welcome flow · FB group link never on the page · keeps the community small + protects signal-to-noise.

Design language

Matches balanzi.com

Same system as balanzi.com/our-story: 480px max-width single column, white background, italic-on-separate-line headlines, dark voice sections breaking up the white. Inter only. Hex palette: #FFFFFF / #1C1C1C / #6B6560 / #E8E3DC / #AAAAAA. No numbered sections — kept clean, not editorial-heavy.
