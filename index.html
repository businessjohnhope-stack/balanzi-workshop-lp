A landing page for The Workshop — BALANZI's prospect community. The page captures an email; the invite arrives only by inbox (the FB group link is never on the page). Three structural directions below, each testing a different value proposition. Same brand voice. Same hard rule: anti-sales, signal-grounded, no fluff.

Brief · what to know before reviewing

What is The Workshop?

The prospect community for BALANZI. A private channel from the two founders — the vision, decisions, mistakes, and the answers a careful buyer would want before purchasing. It's the anti-sales positioning of a brand community: not VIP, not perks, not influencer drops. Just the work.

The conversion architecture

  • Page captures email only
  • Invite link arrives via email (not visible on the page)
  • This keeps the community small + protects the signal-to-noise ratio
  • Email becomes the first Workshop letter — Klaviyo welcome flow seeds the relationship

Brand voice constraints

  • Calm, editorial, anti-sales — no urgency, no discount language
  • Self-Made Man audience (78% male, mean age 50, careful with money)
  • Signal-grounded copy only (verbatim quote / time-elapsed proof / specific provable claim / canonical brand line)
  • Italic accent on a separate line (font:300 italic, color #8A857E)
  • Palette: warm off-white #F4F2EE / deep ink #1C1C1C
  • Inter font only
  • Banned words: premium, luxury, exclusive, lifetime guarantee, won't fade

What's NOT in scope yet

  • Form submission backend (currently inert — wire to Klaviyo on selection)
  • Klaviyo welcome flow templates (separate workstream)
  • Mobile fine-tuning past 600px breakpoint
