Three layouts.One conversion goal.

A landing page for The Workshop — BALANZI's prospect community. The page captures an email; the invite arrives only by inbox. Three structural directions, all in the balanzi.com design system. Same brand voice. Same hard rule: anti-sales, signal-grounded, no fluff.
Brief · what to know

What is The Workshop?

The prospect community for BALANZI. A private channel from the founders — the vision, decisions, mistakes, and the answers a careful buyer would want before purchasing. It's the anti-sales positioning of a brand community: not VIP, not perks, not influencer drops. Just the work.

The conversion architecture: Page captures email only. Invite arrives via email (not visible on the page). Keeps the community small + protects the signal-to-noise ratio. Email becomes the first Workshop letter — Klaviyo welcome flow seeds the relationship.

Design language

Matches balanzi.com

Same design system as balanzi.com/our-story: 480px max-width single column, white background, numbered sections with massive bg numerals, italic-on-separate-line headlines, dark voice sections breaking up the white. Inter font only. Hex palette: #FFFFFF / #1C1C1C / #6B6560 / #E8E3DC / #F5F5F5 / #AAAAAA.
