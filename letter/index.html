May 2026 · The Workshop
To whoever's reading this —

Here's the honest version.Plainly said.

We don't have an atelier. There's no marble counter, no soft-jazz showroom, no creative director. There's a desk, a factory we talk to every day, a few notebooks, and the next chain we're testing on someone's wrist.

That's the Workshop.

The 12mm we're testing this month. Two rounds of polish failed. Round three is in hand.

We built it because every week we have conversations we wish more people could overhear. Why we stopped working with a supplier. Why we killed a prototype at 80%. What we'd actually tell a friend before he picks his first chain.

Those conversations sit in our DMs and disappear. The Workshop is where they get written down.

What you'll find inside, in plain terms:

  • The prototype we walked away from at 80% complete.
  • The clasp that failed inspection and what changed in the next sample.
  • The supplier we stopped working with after a six-month trial.
  • The questions we answer directly before you'd think to ask.
  • Reading recs, music we play while we work, occasional opinions you didn't ask for.

One letter a week. Sometimes two if there's something worth showing. Nothing on weekends. No discounts in your inbox — we don't run them in The Workshop. If we have something to sell you, we'll say so. Otherwise we won't.

The door is your inbox. Drop your email below and we'll send the invite. Please don't forward it. The Workshop stays useful by staying small.

If that sounds like the wrong place for you, that's fine. We'll be here either way.

Yours,
The BALANZI founders
London · the desk · the factory
