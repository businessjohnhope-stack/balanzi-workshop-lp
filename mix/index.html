We started a place.It's called The Workshop.

A letter · May 2026

To whoever's reading this —

Here's the honest version.Plainly said.

We don't have an atelier. There's no marble counter, no soft-jazz showroom, no creative director.

There's a desk, a factory we talk to every day, a few notebooks, and the next chain we're testing on someone's wrist.

That's the Workshop.

We built it because every week we have conversations we wish more people could overhear. Why we stopped working with a supplier. Why we killed a prototype at 80%.

What we'd actually tell a friend before he picks his first chain.

Those conversations sit in our DMs and disappear. The Workshop is where they get written down.

What you'll find

The prototype we walked away fromat 80% complete.

The weight was right. The finish wasn't. Three rounds of polish failed inspection. We share the call, the cost, and what changed in the next sample.

The clasp

The clasp thatfailed inspection.

Six clasps shipped with a tolerance issue we didn't catch. The conversation that ended it — written down, start to finish. The clasp is where most of the decisions live.

  • The chains we ship — and the ones we killed. The 12mm in testing now: two rounds of polish failed, round three is in hand.
  • The supplier we stopped working with. Six-month trial. Two missed deadlines. One QA dispute. The full record.
  • The questions we answer directly before you'd think to ask them.

No discounts in your inbox.

We don't run them in The Workshop. If we have something to sell you, we'll say so. Otherwise we won't. One letter a week. Sometimes two, if there's something worth showing.
