01 · The Vision

What we'reactually building.

Not a fine-jewelry house. Not a hype brand. A workshop — distributed across two founders, a factory, and a few hundred decisions a month. The Workshop is where we share what we'd tell a friend before he picks his first chain.

"Nobody has innovated in stainless steel. That's what we're trying to change." — Josef
02 · The Decisions

The chains we shipand the ones we killed.

The prototype we walked away from at 80%. The clasp that failed inspection. The supplier we stopped working with after a six-month trial. We share the call we made, what it cost us, and what we'd do differently.

03 · The Mistakes

What we got wrong,said plainly.

Fourteen people told us our chains were fake last month. We still don't care — but we listened to what was underneath the question. We share the friction, the bad reviews, the inspection failures. Not as crisis comms. As craft.
